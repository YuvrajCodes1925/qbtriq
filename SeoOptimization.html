Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our SEO services

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy, but you can typically begin to see initial improvements within 3-6 months. Significant results often take 6-12 months, depending on your industry competition, website history, and the scope of the optimization required. We provide regular progress reports so you can track improvements throughout the process.

What makes QBtriq's SEO services different?

We take a holistic, data-driven approach to SEO that combines technical expertise with creative content strategies. Unlike agencies that use cookie-cutter methods, we develop customized SEO plans based on your specific industry, competition, and business goals. We also emphasize transparent reporting and maintain ethical "white hat" practices that ensure sustainable, long-term results.

Do you guarantee first-page rankings?

No reputable SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings, as search engines constantly update their algorithms. However, we have a proven track record of significantly improving rankings for our clients. We focus on sustainable strategies that deliver long-term results rather than quick fixes that could harm your site in the future.

How do you measure SEO success?

We track multiple metrics including keyword rankings, organic traffic growth, conversion rates, bounce rates, time on site, and ultimately, return on investment. Our comprehensive monthly reports provide full transparency into your campaign's performance. We also establish clear KPIs at the beginning of our partnership to ensure we're aligned on what success looks like for your business.

Do you handle local SEO?

Yes, we specialize in local SEO for businesses targeting customers in specific geographic areas. Our local SEO services include Google Business Profile optimization, local citation building, review management, and location-specific keyword targeting to help you dominate local search results and attract more customers from your community.
